DREAM BEACH DESTINATION

Allow me to introduce myself and my dream beach destinations across the Philippines.
Whether into surfing, snorkeling, or just relaxing at the shore, the Philippines offers a variety of pristine beaches.

Boracay Island (Aklan)

Boracay Island is one of my dream beach destinations because of its crystal-clear waters,
and Boracay is also one of the top tourist destinations in the Philippines.
Boracay Island is famous for its stunning white sand beaches, particularly White Beach,
which is known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant nightlife.
The island offers a range of activities, from water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving to relaxation spots and local markets.





Things to do in Boracay:

  • Island hopping
  • Parasailing
  • Sunset watching at White Beach

El Nido (Palawan)

El Nido is a perfect destination because of its dramatic limestone cliffs and turquoise waters.
Known for its hidden lagoons and snorkeling spots, it's my favorite beach destination among the rest.
El Nido, Palawan, is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, featuring dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, and stunning lagoons.





Top activities in El Nido:

  • Kayaking in Big Lagoon
  • Snorkeling at Miniloc Island
  • Scuba diving at Bacuit Bay

Siargao Island (Surigao del Norte)

Siargao Island is on the list because Siargao offers laid-back island vibes with stunning beaches.
It's a perfect spot for those seeking a peaceful getaway. Siargao Island is famous for its beautiful beaches and surf culture,
particularly Cloud 9, which attracts surfers from around the world.





To do's in Siargao:

  • Island hopping at Daku Island
  • Swimming
  • Sunset watching

Bantayan Island (Cebu)

Bantayan Island is a hidden gem in Cebu with calm, turquoise waters and long stretches of white sand.
It's perfect for a quiet retreat, away from the busy city life. Bantayan Island in Cebu is a popular getaway known for its serene beaches and vibrant local culture.





Best things to do in Bantayan:

  • Beach camping at Paradise Beach
  • Visiting Ogtong Cave
  • Snorkeling in nearby islands

Camiguin Island

Known as the "Island Born of Fire", Camiguin is famous for its volcanic landscapes and stunning beaches.
White Island, a sandbar near the main island, offers beautiful sunrise and sunset views.





Activities in Camiguin:

  • Explore White Island
  • Visit Katibawasan Falls
  • Sunrise and Sunset watching
